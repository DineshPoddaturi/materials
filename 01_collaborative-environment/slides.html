Tools for collaborating in teams

A test case

The dataset brfss_iowa.csv (linked from website) contains 6227 records from the Behavioral Risk Factor Surveillance System (BRFSS) for Iowans.

The Behavioral Risk Factor Surveillance System (BRFSS) is the nation’s premier system of health-related telephone surveys that collect state data about U.S. residents regarding their health-related risk behaviors.

It is conducted annually by the Center for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC).

Your turn

You are asked to investigate the relationship between height and weight of Iowans based on the 2015 BRFSS records (see course website for data). (The variables WEIGHT2 and HEIGHT3 are collected during the interview)

In the next 20 mins:

  1. Use your favorite software (IT DOES NOT NEED TO BE R!!!) to come up with a graphical summary of the relationship between weight and height (fix potential problems in the data!).
  2. Write a short report of your findings.

Your turn

In the next 10 mins:

  1. Introduce yourself to your neighbor.
  2. Exchange reports - BUT DO NOT TALK ABOUT THE DATA OR THE ANALYSIS (yet)
  3. Repeat your neighbor’s analysis with the data for Minnesota (brfss_mn.csv)
